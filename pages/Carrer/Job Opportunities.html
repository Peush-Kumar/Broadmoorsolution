- Business Analyst (Entry-Level)

Date Posted: 03/15/2023
Projected Close: 06/15/2023

Job Description:

. Responsible for coordinating and planning development timelines with the IS technical and development teams to
provide delivery dates to the business.

. Conceptual and analytical thinking – must possess the ability to extract, analyze, and document complex business and
technical requirements or strategies.

. Develop all requirements; Identify and analyze business needs and coordinate between stakeholder groups to address them and deliver prompt, efficient, quality service.

. Establish, update, and communicate Project plans to all team members and relevant business stakeholders.

. Facilitate meetings with business users, IS technical and developer teams. In addition, prepare for change management
and follow the formalized change management (CM) process.

. Develop a deep understanding of the business process area and the current application landscape to propose
simplification or improvement opportunities.

. Oversee User Acceptance testing and be the intermediary between the business and the IS technical and development
teams.

. Leverage data and facts to identify patterns, uses judgment to form conclusions that may challenge conventional
wisdom, and focuses on the issues to identify high leverage intervention points and strategies

. Responsible for testing initiatives, which include development unit testing and system testing in multiple environments
prior to the project being tested by the business.

. Oversee production implementation and ensure all necessary documentation is approved.

. Responsible for creating and documenting test strategy, test scripts.

Required:

. Highly Motivated Team player with excellent verbal and written communication

. Detail oriented and deadline-driven

. Ability to work independently

. Critical and Analytical thinking

. Demonstrated problem solving skills

. Good Organization and time management skills

. Proficient in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Teams)

. Strong presentation skills (preferred)

Education Requirements:

. Senior Year or Graduate Student in: Business Analytics, Business Administration, Business, Leadership
